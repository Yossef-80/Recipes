ABOUT MEAL : Fattoush salad is a vibrant Levantine dish, a medley of fresh vegetables, herbs, and crispy pita bread. Typically, it includes tomatoes, cucumbers, radishes, and lettuce, dressed with a tangy mix of lemon juice, olive oil, and sumac. Its defining feature is toasted or fried pieces of pita bread, adding crunch and flavor. Often garnished with parsley and mint, it offers a refreshing contrast of textures and tastes. Fattoush is both nutritious and flavorful, making it a beloved staple in Middle Eastern cuisine, perfect for a light and satisfying meal.
